Mississippi Chicken Recipe

Mississippi chicken is simple to make with just four ingredients for juicy, tangy shredded chicken that's delicious in buns or served over mashed potatoes or rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Dry Ranch Dressing Mix
  • Pepperoncini Peppers
  • Pepper Juice
  • Unsalted Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Season chicken with ranch seasoning mix
  3. Add pepperoncini peppers and 1/4 cup reserved pepper juice
  4. Top with sliced butter and pour in 1/2 cup water
